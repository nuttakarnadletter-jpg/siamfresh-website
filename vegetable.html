Thai Herbs & Aromatics

Thai herbs and aromatics are prized for their vibrant fragrance, distinctive flavour profiles, and essential role in authentic Southeast Asian cuisine.

Siam Fresh selects lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, Thai basil, coriander, and other key aromatics from GAP-certified farms. Each batch is harvested at peak freshness and handled under strict food safety standards from field to cold-chain export delivery.

Why It Stands Out

Carefully graded for aroma intensity, leaf condition, colour, and uniformity — with full traceability from farm to shipment.

Tropical Favourites

Tropical Vegetables

Thailand's tropical climate produces a diverse range of vegetables valued for their texture, flavour, and versatility in global kitchens.

Siam Fresh supplies baby corn, long beans, Thai eggplant, okra, winged beans, and other tropical varieties selected for size consistency, skin quality, and freshness. Post-harvest handling preserves texture and shelf life for reliable arrival quality.

Why It Stands Out

Inspected and graded for maturity, appearance, firmness, and consistency under controlled packing and cold-chain management.

Crisp & Vibrant

Leafy Greens

Thai leafy greens are valued for their crisp texture, vibrant colour, and nutritional profile — ideal for retail, food service, and processing markets.

Each batch is harvested at the appropriate stage of maturity to support natural freshness, attractive appearance, and dependable shelf life. Greens are pre-cooled, packed, and shipped under controlled temperature to maintain quality from farm to destination.

Why It Stands Out

Selected for leaf integrity, colour, cleanliness, and uniformity with residue monitoring aligned to export market requirements.

Popular Varieties

Chinese kale, morning glory, lettuce, cos lettuce, Chinese cabbage, and water spinach.
