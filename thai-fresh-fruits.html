Thailand's Leading Export Durian

Premium Monthong

Monthong (Golden Pillow) is Thailand's most widely exported durian variety, renowned for its exceptional balance of flavour, texture, and consistency.

Its thick, creamy flesh, naturally sweet profile, and relatively mild aroma have made it the preferred choice for international markets, offering a refined eating experience for both first-time consumers and seasoned durian enthusiasts.

At Siam Fresh, our Premium Monthong is sourced from carefully selected orchards with trees aged over 28 years. Combined with extended on-tree ripening, this allows the fruit to develop greater flavour complexity, a smoother texture, and a naturally richer taste.

Why Siam Fresh Durian Stands Out

Quality Begins Long Before Harvest.

At Siam Fresh, we source from orchards with durian trees aged over 28 years. Mature trees develop deeper root systems, stronger nutrient uptake, and more consistent fruit quality over time.

Every batch is monitored through strict quality control measures, with Dry Matter content exceeding 32% serving as a key benchmark for premium Monthong selection.

Extended on-tree ripening allows the fruit to develop richer flavour, smoother texture, and a naturally more satisfying eating experience.

The Queen of Fruits

Premium Mangosteen

Mangosteen (Garcinia mangostana L.) is widely regarded as the "Queen of Fruits", celebrated for its delicate balance of sweetness and acidity, snow-white flesh, and exceptionally refreshing taste.

Its unique flavour profile and premium appearance have made it one of Southeast Asia's most prized tropical fruits.

Grown in Thailand's renowned fruit-producing regions, Premium Mangosteen from Siam Fresh is carefully selected to deliver the freshness, flavour, and consistency expected by international markets.

Thailand's Signature Golden Mango

Premium Nam Dok Mai Mango

Nam Dok Mai Mango is one of Thailand’s most celebrated mango varieties, admired for its elegant golden appearance, smooth low-fibre flesh, delicate floral aroma, and naturally sweet taste.

Its refined texture, balanced sweetness, and premium presentation have made it a preferred choice for international fresh-fruit markets, offering a graceful eating experience for both everyday consumers and premium fruit buyers.

At Siam Fresh, our Premium Nam Dok Mai Mango is sourced from carefully selected orchards in Thailand’s key mango-producing regions. Each fruit is harvested at the appropriate stage of maturity to support its natural sweetness, smooth texture, attractive colour development, and reliable arrival quality for export markets.
